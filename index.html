Retro Gesture Snake

How to Play

Control the snake with finger gestures:

  • Index finger up: Move left
  • Pinky up: Move right
  • Index + middle up: Move up
  • All fingers down: Move down

Eat green food (10 points) or yellow stars (10 points + speed boost). Avoid obstacles!
